Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app really ad-free?

Yes. The core experience is completely free of ads. Premium removes any remaining distractions entirely.

How does the Family Tree work?

You create a private family space, invite relatives, and add photos, prayers, and milestones. Future generations can continue the story.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your prayers and family content are private by default. We use industry-standard security and never sell your data.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. Bible plans, your journal entries, and Family Tree content are available offline. Changes sync when you reconnect.
